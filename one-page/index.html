NERISSA YAP

Fashion Stylist, Model, & Designer Multimedia Producer I’m a Fine Arts graduate with a degree in Advertising Arts. My projects focus on lifestyle and the different stories and characters it brings about. I am a storyteller, and I convery these narratives through fashion designing, modelling, photography, and filming. I am both a minimalist and a maximalist with a strong eye for detail. My aesthetics are both classical and contemporary, and still, I welcome opportunities that challenge my artistry.
